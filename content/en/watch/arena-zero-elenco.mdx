## How to Unlock and Progress Your Roster

Expanding your **arena zero elenco** is a straightforward process, though it requires consistent play or strategic resource management. 1.  **Select a Contract:** Go to the "Heroes" tab and activate the contract for the character you wish to unlock.

4.  **Tier 10 Rewards:** Completing the full contract grants exclusive cosmetic skins and weapon charms.

## Advanced Character Customization: The Talent Tree

One of the standout features of the **arena zero elenco** in 2026 is the revamped Talent Tree system. Unlike traditional shooters where characters are static, Arena Zero allows you to fine-tune your hero's abilities to match your personal playstyle.

### The Three Branches of Power
Every character has access to three distinct talent branches:
*   **Power:** Enhances raw damage and ability lethality.
*   **Utility:** Reduces cooldowns and increases movement speed.
*   **Fortitude:** Increases health pools and damage resistance.

For example, a "Utility-focused" Sora is significantly faster and can deploy her scouting drones 30% more often, making her an elite-tier gatherer of information. Conversely, a "Power-focused" Sora deals bonus damage to enemies she has personally spotted, turning her into a deadly assassin.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Talent points are limited per character level. Avoid spreading your points too thin across all three branches; it is usually better to specialize in two.
