### Innovative Design Choices
The "Basil Arena" itself was designed to be circular. This wasn't just an aesthetic choice to pay homage to Roman gladiators; it was a functional decision. A circular structure allowed the AI models to maintain environmental consistency more easily across multiple camera angles, ensuring that the background didn't "hallucinate" or change unexpectedly during high-speed action sequences.
