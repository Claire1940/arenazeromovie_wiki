## The Story and Setting of Planet Zero

The narrative of Arena Zero follows Leo, an ordinary human who finds himself suddenly transported to a cosmic gladiatorial pit. The series utilizes a "system-based" logic often seen in RPGs and LitRPG novels. Upon arrival, Leo is forced to choose a starting item under a strict 10-second timer.

This decision sets the tone for the series—strategy and meta-gaming are just as important as raw power. The setting, Planet Zero, acts as a hub for the "Thousand Worlds," where one champion per world must fight to prevent their planet's extinction.
