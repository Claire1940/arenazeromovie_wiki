## Combat Mechanics and Inventory

A unique aspect of **Arena Zero Season 1** is the integration of gaming "meta" into the story. When Leo arrives, he is given 10 seconds to choose a starting item. Defying traditional logic, he passes over a sword in favor of high-tech sneakers, betting on movement speed over raw attack power.

### Key Equipment and Abilities
*   **Movement Speed Sneakers:** Leo's primary tool. While they offer no defense, they allow him to exploit the "hitboxes" of larger, slower enemies.
*   **Healing Slime:** A biological medical system that stops bleeding and seals injuries, though it is described as incredibly uncomfortable to use.
*   **The Symbol:** A mysterious UI element that appears when Leo unlocks a new ability, signaling a "speedrun" tactic or a tactical shift.

## Survival Strategies in the Arena

To survive the first encounter with the 7-foot Ogre from Planet Nid, Leo demonstrates several core strategies that define the "gamer" approach to intergalactic combat:

1.  **Exploit Mass:** Larger enemies have significant momentum. By utilizing "Movement Speed," Leo forces the opponent to overcommit to strikes, leading them to get stuck or lose balance.
2.  **Monitor the Death Timer:** Never let a fight drag on. The 3-minute clock is an environmental hazard that must be managed.
3.  **Identify Hitboxes:** Leo notes that the "hitboxes are awful," implying that certain visual parts of an enemy may not be vulnerable, while others are susceptible to "glitched" or high-damage strikes.
