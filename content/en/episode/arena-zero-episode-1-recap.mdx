The episode opens with a jarring transition from a mundane, somewhat depressing reality to the neon-soaked brutality of Planet Zero. ## Combat Analysis: Leo vs. Heat

The first battle of the series pits Leo against Heat, a massive warrior from the planet Nid. Heat is a powerhouse characterized by immense physical mass and short legs. The fight serves as a tutorial for the audience on how combat works in Arena Zero.

### The Mechanics of Healing
During the fight, Leo sustains injuries that require immediate attention. Hokei introduces a "healing slime" system. While the slime appears sentient and somewhat disgusting to Leo, it serves as a temporary medical fix to stop bleeding. It is made clear that if Leo dies here, he "planet dives," meaning everyone he knows on Earth will perish alongside him.

### The Strategy
Leo realizes that he cannot win a war of attrition against Heat. After losing 5 HP to a stray hit, he notices a timer (3 minutes) and a symbol indicating a new ability or environmental trigger. By utilizing the speed from his sneakers, Leo executes a "speedrun" strategy. He lures Heat into a position where the giant’s own mass becomes a liability. Once Heat is downed, his short legs and massive weight keep him "stuck," allowing Leo to claim victory.
