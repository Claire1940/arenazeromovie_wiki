## Mastering the "Heat" System

The "Heat" system is the core of **arena zero episode 5** gameplay. It isn't just a damage buff; it is a tactical necessity. When the Heat gauge is full, your attacks gain "Impact" properties. This is the only way to reliably stop the boss from continuing his attack animations. 

*   **Blue Heat:** Standard damage buff.
*   **Red Heat:** Maximum power, allows for "Breaker" moves.
*   **Heat Decay:** The gauge drops quickly if you are not actively engaging the enemy.

Players who struggle with this episode often make the mistake of playing too defensively. If you back away to heal, your Heat gauge will reset, making the boss virtually impossible to stagger. You must maintain a "controlled aggression" to keep the pressure on.
