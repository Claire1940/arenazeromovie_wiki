## Plot Summary: Earth’s Unwilling Champion

### The Tournament Rules
*   **One Champion Per World:** Each planet must provide a single warrior.
*   **Planetary Stakes:** If a champion loses their battle, their entire home world is "done"—completely erased from existence.
*   **The 480-Hour Cycle:** After a victory, warriors are given a 480-hour rest and recovery period before their next life-or-death encounter.

Leo’s first opponent is a massive warrior from Planet Nid, often referred to in production notes as the "7-foot Ogre." Despite the availability of traditional weapons like swords, Leo makes the unconventional choice of selecting high-tech sneakers as his primary "item." This decision highlights the meta-gaming influence of the series, where movement speed is prioritized over raw attack power.
