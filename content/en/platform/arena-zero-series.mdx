## Combat Mechanics and the Sneaker Meta

One of the most intriguing aspects of the **arena zero series** is how it incorporates RPG and action-game mechanics into its cinematography. When Leo is forced to choose an item for his first battle, he is presented with a 10-second timer. While most would choose a sword, Leo opts for high-tech sneakers, claiming that "movement speed is always meta."

This decision turns the first battle into a "speedrun" style encounter. Leo uses the sneakers not just for evasion, but as a kinetic weapon. The combat system includes:
*   **HP Bars:** Visual indicators of health for both Leo and his opponents.
*   **Death Timers:** A 3-minute clock that forces aggression; if neither fighter wins before time expires, both are eliminated (and their planets destroyed).
*   **Status Effects:** Healing slime is used to temporarily stop bleeding, though its "moving" nature is unsettling to the protagonist.
*   **Ability Symbols:** New combat abilities unlock as Leo gains experience within the arena environment.
