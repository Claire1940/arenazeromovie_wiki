1.  **Character Sketching**: The AI is given a prompt based on the planet's environment (e.g., Earth, Nid, or Planet Zero).

3.  **Action Mapping**: Combat moves are generated based on physics engines, ensuring that movements like the "healing slime" application or "speedrun" maneuvers look fluid.
4.  **Consistency Check**: A secondary AI pass ensures that the character looks the same across different lighting conditions and action intensities.
