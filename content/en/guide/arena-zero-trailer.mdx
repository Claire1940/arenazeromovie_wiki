## The Lore of Planet Zero: An Intergalactic Survival Game

According to the details revealed in the **arena zero trailer**, Planet Zero serves as the ultimate neutral ground for the "Intergalactic Fight." This is not merely a sport; it is a cosmic culling process. The lore establishes that there are a thousand worlds, each with its own unique characteristics, but they all converge here to settle the fate of their civilizations.

The rules are deceptively simple but devastatingly final:
1. Each world selects one champion to represent them.
2. If the champion wins, their world continues to exist.
3. If the champion loses, their entire world is "done"—wiped from existence.

Interestingly, the trailer mentions that Earth has been in this position before. Approximately 66 million years ago, Earth’s previous champion failed, leading to a global extinction event. This suggests that the "Arena Zero" tournament is a recurring cycle that dictates the evolutionary history of the universe. In 2026, the mantle has fallen to Leo, a man who appears more concerned with his missing clothes than the weight of the world on his shoulders.
