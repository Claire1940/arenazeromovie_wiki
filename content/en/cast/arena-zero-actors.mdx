1.  **Identity Locking:** A "Soul ID" is created for each actor. This acts as a digital DNA that the AI references for every frame.
2.  **Character Sheets:** Much like a traditional costume or makeup department, these sheets lock in accessories (like Leo's sneakers), clothing textures, and physical proportions.
3.  **Environmental Integration:** Using "Prop Sheets" and "Location Sheets," the team ensures the actors interact realistically with their surroundings, such as the institutional blue of a locker room or the dust of the arena floor.
4.  **Vocal Synthesis:** Voices are generated using advanced audio synthesis, splitting tracks into separate lanes to apply specific emotional inflections like weariness or joy.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid over-prompting the environment during the casting phase. Focus first on the "feeling" of the character (e.g., "haunted by past failures") and let the AI model suggest the subtle facial details to avoid a cluttered or unrealistic frame.
