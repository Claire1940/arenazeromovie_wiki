## The World of Planet Zero

The setting of Arena Zero is a circular stadium designed to pay homage to classic gladiator movies while maintaining "location consistency" through its AI-generated architecture. This circular structure is functional for the **arena zero main character**, as it prevents him from being cornered easily.

### The Stakes of the Tournament
*   **One Champion Per World:** Each planet sends its best (or in Earth's case, a random pick) to fight.
*   **The Penalty:** If a champion loses, their entire world is "done"—erased from existence.
*   **The Rest Period:** Battles are spaced out by 480 hours, allowing for recovery and training.

The creation of the **arena zero main character** and his world was achieved through a revolutionary AI pipeline using tools like Higsfield's Seedance 2.0.
